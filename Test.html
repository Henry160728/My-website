1. Introduction to Pump Fun Coin

What is Pump Fun?
Pump.fun is a Solana-based token launchpad that enables users to create and trade memecoins with minimal barriers to entry. The platform has revolutionized how new tokens are launched and traded in the crypto ecosystem.

Pump.fun operates on a unique bonding curve mechanism where:

  • Anyone can create a token with minimal cost (~0.02 SOL)
  • Tokens are initially traded on the platform's bonding curve
  • Once a token reaches a market cap threshold (typically $69K), liquidity migrates to Raydium DEX
  • Early traders can benefit from significant price movements

3. Step-by-Step Trading Guide

1 Set Up Your Wallet
  • Install Phantom or Solflare wallet (browser extension or mobile app)
  • Create a new wallet or import existing one
  • Secure your seed phrase in a safe location
  • Fund wallet with SOL (minimum 0.5-1 SOL recommended for trading)
[SCREENSHOT: Phantom Wallet Setup Interface]
2 Connect to Pump.fun
  • Visit pump.fun official website
  • Click "Connect Wallet" in top right corner
  • Select your wallet provider (Phantom/Solflare)
  • Approve connection request in wallet popup
3 Research Tokens
  • Browse trending tokens on homepage
  • Check token metrics: market cap, holder count, volume
  • Review social media presence and community engagement
  • Analyze creator's wallet history for rug pull indicators
  • Use tools like Solscan or Birdeye for additional data
[SCREENSHOT: Pump.fun Token Discovery Page]
4 Execute Your Trade
  • Click on selected token to open trading interface
  • Enter SOL amount you want to spend
  • Review slippage settings (typically 5-15% for volatile tokens)
  • Set priority fee for faster transaction (higher fee = faster execution)
  • Click "Buy" and confirm transaction in wallet
  • Wait for confirmation (usually 1-3 seconds)
5 Monitor Your Position
  • Track token price on Pump.fun interface
  • Set price alerts if available
  • Watch for migration to Raydium (graduation event)
  • Monitor holder distribution changes
6 Exit Strategy
  • Decide exit points before entering (take profit/stop loss)
  • Use same interface to sell tokens back to SOL
  • Consider partial exits to lock in profits
  • After Raydium migration, trade on DEX for potentially better liquidity

5. Risk Management Strategies

⚠ Critical Warning: Trading Pump.fun tokens involves extremely high risk. Most tokens fail to maintain value. Only invest what you can afford to lose completely.

Position Sizing

  • Maximum Per Trade: Never risk more than 2-5% of your trading capital on a single token
  • Diversification: Spread risk across multiple tokens rather than going all-in
  • Reserve Capital: Keep at least 50% in SOL for opportunities and gas fees

Entry Strategy

Tiered Entry Approach
  • Enter 30% of planned position on initial discovery
  • Add 30% if token shows strength and community growth
  • Final 40% only if fundamental metrics confirm thesis

Exit Strategy

Scenario Action Reasoning
2x Profit Sell 50% of position Lock in initial investment
5x Profit Sell additional 25% Secure significant gains
10x+ Profit Trail remaining position Let winners run with protection

Essential Risk Controls

1. Stop Loss: Always have a predetermined exit point (-15% to -25%)
2. Time Limits: Don't hold losing positions hoping for recovery
3. Emotional Discipline: Stick to your plan regardless of FOMO
4. Transaction Speed: Use adequate priority fees to ensure order execution
5. Wallet Security: Use separate wallets for trading vs. long-term holdings

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Chasing pumps after 100%+ moves
  • Falling for coordinated pump signals in Telegram groups
  • Trading with funds needed for essential expenses
  • Revenge trading after losses
  • Ignoring fundamental research due to hype
  • Not setting appropriate slippage (too low = failed transactions)
Scam Awareness: Be vigilant against rug pulls, honeypots, and social engineering. Verify all information independently and never share your seed phrase.
